Hamburger

Description

A hamburger is a classic sandwich with a grilled beef patty, fresh lettuce, tomato, and cheese between toasted buns. Condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayo add flavor, and toppings like onions or bacon create endless variations. Paired with fries, it's a beloved meal worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patty
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup (optional)

Steps

  1. Shape ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill or pan-fry the patties until cooked to your preferred doneness.
  3. Toast the buns briefly on the grill or in a pan.
  4. Assemble with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments on the buns.
