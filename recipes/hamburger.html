Description

Shawarma is a Middle Eastern dish of thinly sliced, marinated meat, slow-cooked on a vertical spit. Wrapped in pita with veggies, pickles, and garlic or tahini sauce, it has a rich, savory flavor. Shawarmaâ€™s spices and juicy texture make it a popular street food.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patty
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup (optional)

Steps

  1. Shape ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill or pan-fry the patties until cooked to your preferred doneness.
  3. Toast the buns briefly on the grill or in a pan.
  4. Assemble with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments on the buns.
