Shawarma

Description

A hamburger is a classic sandwich with a grilled beef patty, fresh lettuce, tomato, and cheese between toasted buns. Condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayo add flavor, and toppings like onions or bacon create endless variations. Paired with fries, it's a beloved meal worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Chicken or lamb
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Pita bread
  • Tahini sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix teff flour, water, and a pinch of salt, and let the batter ferment for 1-3 days.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour a thin layer of batter onto the skillet and cook until bubbles form and the top is set.
  4. Remove from the pan and let it cool before serving with stews or curries.
