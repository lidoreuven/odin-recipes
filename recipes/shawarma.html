Shawarma

Description

A hamburger is a classic sandwich with a grilled beef patty, fresh lettuce, tomato, and cheese between toasted buns. Condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayo add flavor, and toppings like onions or bacon create endless variations. Paired with fries, it's a beloved meal worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Chicken or lamb
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Pita bread
  • Tahini sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Marinate thin slices of chicken or lamb with garlic, cumin, and paprika for at least 1 hour.
  2. Grill or roast the meat until cooked and tender.
  3. Slice the cooked meat thinly and place it in pita bread.
  4. Add vegetables, pickles, and tahini sauce if desired.
  5. Wrap tightly and enjoy.
