Shawarma

Description

Shawarma is a Middle Eastern dish of thinly sliced, marinated meat, slow-cooked on a vertical spit. Wrapped in pita with veggies, pickles, and garlic or tahini sauce, it has a rich, savory flavor. Shawarmaâ€™s spices and juicy texture make it a popular street food.

Ingredients

  • Chicken or lamb
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Pita bread
  • Tahini sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Marinate thin slices of chicken or lamb with garlic, cumin, and paprika for at least 1 hour.
  2. Grill or roast the meat until cooked and tender.
  3. Slice the cooked meat thinly and place it in pita bread.
  4. Add vegetables, pickles, and tahini sauce if desired.
  5. Wrap tightly and enjoy.
