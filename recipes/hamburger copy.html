Ingredients

  • Ground beef patty
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup (optional)

Steps

  1. Shape ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill or pan-fry the patties until cooked to your preferred doneness.
  3. Toast the buns briefly on the grill or in a pan.
  4. Assemble with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments on the buns.
