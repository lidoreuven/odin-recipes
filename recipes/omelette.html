Omelette

Description

An omelette is a classic breakfast dish made by whisking eggs and cooking them in a pan until fluffy and lightly golden. Simple to customize, it can be filled with ingredients like cheese, herbs, or vegetables, giving it added flavor and texture. Light and satisfying, omelettes are enjoyed worldwide and are known for their versatility and ease of preparation, making them perfect for a quick meal at any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (2-3)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Butter or oil (1 tbsp)
  • Cheese, herbs, or veggies (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan, spreading evenly, and cook until edges set.
  4. Add cheese, herbs, or veggies if desired, then fold the omelette in half.
  5. Cook for another minute, then slide onto a plate and serve warm.
