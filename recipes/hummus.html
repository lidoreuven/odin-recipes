Hummus

Description

Hummus is a creamy, flavorful Middle Eastern dip made from blended chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic. Known for its smooth texture and nutty, tangy taste, it’s a popular appetizer or snack, often served with pita bread, vegetables, or as a spread. With its balance of healthy fats, protein, and fiber, hummus is both nutritious and versatile, making it a staple in many Mediterranean dishes.

Ingredients

  • Tahini (¼ cup)
  • Lemon juice (1-2 tbsp)
  • Garlic (1 clove)
  • Chickpeas (1 cup, cooked)

Steps

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, and salt.
  2. Blend until smooth, adding a little water if needed for desired consistency.
  3. Adjust seasoning with more salt or lemon juice as preferred.
  4. Serve with a drizzle of olive oil on top and sprinkle with paprika if desired.
